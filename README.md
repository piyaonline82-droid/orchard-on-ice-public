# Orchard On Ice

  > The pond has frozen over and the orchard's apples are falling! Slide your teddy bear left and
  > right to catch them in the basket and build up combos for big scores. But watch out — a mischievous
  > sprite is dropping anvils, and one of those will smash your basket. How long can you last as the
  > apples fall faster and faster?
  >
  > • Simple one-finger play — just drag to move.
  > • Cozy autumn-sunset orchard on a sheet of ice.
  > • Combos, golden apples, and rising levels.
  > • No ads, no in-app purchases, no internet required — plays fully offline.
