Privacy Policy — Orchard on Ice

Last updated: 11 June 2026

Orchard on Ice does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, no account or login, and it makes no network connections — it works fully offline. The only information it saves is your best score and your sound-on/off preference, which are stored locally on your device (in the app's own storage) so the game remembers them between sessions. This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to us or anyone else, and is removed if you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

Orchard on Ice is designed to be appropriate for all ages, including children, and participates in Google Play's Designed for Families program. The app collects no personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It contains no ads, no in-app purchases, no third-party SDKs, no social features, and no links out of the app, and it makes no network connections. Because we collect no personal information at all, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation provisions for children (GDPR-K). If you are a parent or guardian and have any questions, contact us at the address below.
